Role-based Personas

We have developed a set of personas to give a flavour of the roles HC could fill on a digital project. For each, we have linked some resources that would be of interest.

James

Agile Project Manager and PMO

"I really enjoy working with a wide group of stakeholders to ensure the programme stays focused on priorities, and ultimately delivers maximum value to users"

Background and Role

Typically James works on digital delivery programmes with clients who are involved in digital transformation, managing central governance and planning functions to triage scope with multiple stakeholders from business, technology and design to ensure everyone can commit to deliver programme expectations.

Some of the activities he might undertake include:

  • Co-ordinate scoping and delivery governance forums
  • Track delivery to milestones through Kanban, agile ceremonies, formal forums & plans
  • Help teams to understand wider business priorities
Sound like you? Take a look at
  • Kanban system design
  • Why estimation is hard
  • Scrum - the basics, LinkedIn learning
  • Scrum - advanced, LinkedIn learning
